The background of this img was created with a yellow circle and an orange background. The gradual opacity fade of the yellow circle was achieved with a mask, and the random patterns was created with the smudge tool. My photo was processed with the Stained Glass filter.


No filter was used in this img. Since the photo was a bit dark, I decided to create a fancy atmosphere through image adjustments such as brightness, contrast, exposure and saturation.

I found it is interesting to put myself in one of the displays in the Time Square. The edge was processed with blur tool to make it looks a bit natural.
